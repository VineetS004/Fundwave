About Us

We are a charity organization that helps poor people in need. Through crowdfunding, we connect generous donors with people who need financial assistance for basic necessities, healthcare, education, and more.

Fundwave has emerged as a revolutionary way for individuals and organizations to raise funds for their projects, ideas, and ventures. By leveraging the power of the internet and social media, crowdfunding platforms connect creators with potential backers who share a passion for innovation and creativity. This democratized approach to funding allows anyone with a compelling idea to reach out to a global audience, bypassing traditional financial institutions and their often stringent requirements.
